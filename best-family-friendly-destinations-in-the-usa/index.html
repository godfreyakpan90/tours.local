Best Family Friendly Destinations in the USA – Top 3 List

6 min read
Written by Godfrey

Are you looking for family friendly destinations in the USA for your travel? Here are best family friendly destinations in the USA for summer visit and tours. These family-friendly destinations offer a mix of entertainment, educational experiences, outdoor adventures, and attractions that cater to visitors of all ages, making them ideal choices for memorable family vacations in the USA.

Family Friendly Destinations in the USA

Orlando, Florida

 

Orlando Theme Park Capital of the World

Orlando is also Known as the “Theme Park Capital of the World,” Orlando offers a variety of family-friendly attractions, including Walt Disney World Resort, Universal Orlando Resort, SeaWorld Orlando, and LEGOLAND Florida. There are also numerous water parks, interactive museums, and outdoor activities for families to enjoy.

Orlando Theme Park  for Outdoor Activities

  • Visit Walt Disney World Resort
  • Explore Universal Orlando Resort
  • Enjoy SeaWorld Orlando
  • Experience LEGOLAND Florida

San Diego, California

San Diego boasts a pleasant climate, beautiful beaches, and a range of family-friendly attractions. Families can visit the world-famous San Diego Zoo, explore interactive exhibits at the Birch Aquarium, enjoy the rides at Belmont Park, and explore the historic USS Midway Museum.

  2. Torrey Pines State Natural Reserve
  3. Mission Bay Park
  4. Presidio Park
  5. Old Town San Diego State Historic Park
  6. Mission Trails Regional Park
  7. Cabrillo National Monument
  8. La Jolla Cove and Ellen Browning Scripps Park
  9. Sunset Cliffs Natural Park
  10. Seaport Village and Embarcadero Marina Park
  11. Kate O. Sessions Memorial Park
  12. Cowles Mountain
  13. Waterfront Park
  14. Torrey Pines State Beach
  15. San Diego Botanic Garden
  16. Marian Bear Memorial Park
  17. Santee Lakes Recreation Preserve
  18. Lake Murray
  19. Anza-Borrego Desert State Park (near San Diego)
  20. William Heise County Park (near San Diego)

Washington, D.C.

The nation’s capital is a great destination for families to explore American history and culture. Visit iconic landmarks such as the National Mall, Smithsonian Museums, the Lincoln Memorial, and the White House. There are also interactive museums like the National Air and Space Museum and the International Spy Museum that cater to children.

Washington, D.C. Parks for Outdoor Activities

  1. Rock Creek Park
  2. National Mall and Memorial Parks
  3. Theodore Roosevelt Island
  4. Meridian Hill Park

  6. National Arboretum

  14. Bartholdi Park
  15. The Wharf and Waterfront Park

  20. Lafayette Square

Williamsburg, Virginia

Williamsburg is home to Colonial Williamsburg, a living history museum that allows families to step back in time and experience life in the 18th century. The area also includes Busch Gardens Williamsburg, a thrilling amusement park with rides and shows suitable for all ages.

Williamsburg, Virginia Parks for Outdoor Activities

  1. Colonial National Historical Park
  2. Freedom Park
  3. Waller Mill Park
  4. York River State Park
  5. New Quarter Park
  6. Kiwanis Park
  7. Williamsburg Botanical Garden
  8. Waller Mill Dog Park

Myrtle Beach, South Carolina

Myrtle Beach, South Carolina Parks and Experience for Outdoor Activities

Myrtle Beach offers a wide range of family-friendly activities, including beautiful beaches, amusement parks, mini-golf courses, water parks, and the Myrtle Beach SkyWheel. Families can also visit Ripley’s Aquarium, Broadway at the Beach, and the Myrtle Beach State Park.

Myrtle Beach, South Carolina Parks and Experience for Outdoor Activities

  1. Myrtle Beach State Park
  2. Huntington Beach State Park
  3. Brookgreen Gardens
  4. Myrtle Beach Boardwalk and Promenade
  5. Myrtle Beach State Park Pier
  6. Warbird Park
  7. Vereen Memorial Historical Gardens

Gatlinburg, Tennessee

Gatlinburg, Tennessee Parks and Experience for Outdoor Activities

Located near the Great Smoky Mountains National Park, Gatlinburg offers a natural playground for families. Explore hiking trails, go wildlife spotting, and enjoy outdoor activities like white-water rafting or zip-lining. Families can also visit popular attractions such as Ripley’s Aquarium of the Smokies and the Gatlinburg Space Needle.

Gatlinburg, Tennessee Parks and Experience for Outdoor Activities

  1. Great Smoky Mountains National Park
  2. Roaring Fork Motor Nature Trail
  3. Cades Cove
  4. Smoky Mountains Arts & Crafts Community
  5. Ober Gatlinburg
  6. Gatlinburg Trail
  7. Chimney Tops Trail
  8. Smoky Mountain Ziplines

New York City, New York

New York City, New York Parks for Outdoor Activities

While it may seem like a bustling city, New York City has plenty of family-friendly attractions. Visit iconic landmarks like Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty. Explore interactive museums like the American Museum of Natural History, the Children’s Museum of Manhattan, and the Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum.

New York City, New York Parks for Outdoor Activities

  1. Central Park
  2. Prospect Park
  3. High Line
  4. Bryant Park
  5. Brooklyn Bridge Park
  6. Riverside Park
  7. Flushing Meadows-Corona Park
  8. Battery Park
  9. Hudson River Park
  10. The Cloisters and Fort Tryon Park
  11. Van Cortlandt Park
  12. Forest Park
  13. Pelham Bay Park
  14. Socrates Sculpture Park
  15. Alley Pond Park

Branson, Missouri

Branson, Missouri Parks for Outdoor Activities

Branson is a popular family destination in the heart of the Ozark Mountains. It offers a variety of entertainment options, including live music shows, amusement parks, water parks, and outdoor recreational activities like hiking and boating on Table Rock Lake.

Branson, Missouri Parks for Outdoor Activities

  1. Table Rock State Park
  2. Dogwood Canyon Nature Park
  3. Lakeside Forest Wilderness Area
  4. Moonshine Beach
  5. Ruth and Paul Henning Conservation Area
  6. White Water
  7. Stockstill Park
  8. Busiek State Forest and Wildlife Area
  9. Henning Conservation Area
  10. Stockstill Park
  11. Cooper Creek Resort and Campground
  12. Roark Creek
  13. Branson Landing Fountain Show

Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin

Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin Parks for Outdoor Activities

Known as the “Waterpark Capital of the World,” Wisconsin Dells features a multitude of indoor and outdoor water parks suitable for families. There are also other attractions like mini-golf courses, go-karts, zip-lining, and interactive museums like the Tommy Bartlett Exploratory.

Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin Parks for Outdoor Activities

  1. Mirror Lake State Park
  2. Rocky Arbor State Park
  3. Devil’s Lake State Park
  4. Wisconsin River State Natural Area
  5. Witches Gulch
  6. H.H. Bennett Studio State Historic Site

Outer Banks, North Carolina

Outer Banks, North Carolina Parks for Outdoor Activities

The Outer Banks is a family-friendly destination with beautiful beaches, historic lighthouses, and a relaxed atmosphere. Families can enjoy beach activities, visit the North Carolina Aquarium, climb the Cape Hatteras Lighthouse, and explore the Wright Brothers National Memorial.

Outer Banks, North Carolina Parks for Outdoor Activities

  1. Cape Hatteras National Seashore
  2. Jockey’s Ridge State Park
  3. Nags Head Woods Ecological Preserve
  4. Pea Island National Wildlife Refuge
  5. Kitty Hawk Woods Coastal Reserve
  6. Currituck Banks Reserve
